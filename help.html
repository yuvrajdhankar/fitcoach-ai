Quick recovery rules

Recovery support makes the product feel more complete and more useful for real people.

Sleep first

Try to keep 7 to 9 hours of quality sleep, especially when strength volume or cardio load rises.

Easy-day strategy

After a hard day, keep the next session easier or more technical instead of stacking fatigue.

Mobility reset

Five to eight minutes of mobility after sessions can help you recover and move better.

Help topics

These expandable sections echo the beginner guide screenshot while matching the new design language.

Getting started at the gym Guide
Start with basic movement patterns, log simple workouts consistently, and focus on form before chasing intensity. Use the workout page to capture only the details you can track honestly every week.
How to perform exercises better Technique
Choose a manageable load, control the eccentric phase, and leave 1 to 2 reps in reserve on most sets if you are still learning. Add notes to your workout log when something feels unstable or especially strong.
Rest and recovery Recovery
Recovery is just as important as your workouts. Keep hydration high, walk on lighter days, stretch regularly, and avoid pushing hard every session if your sleep and energy are dropping.
How to use FitCoach AI daily Workflow
Open the dashboard in the morning, log training after your session, add one meal check-in, and review your goals once per week. That simple loop already makes the app useful as a personal fitness command center.
